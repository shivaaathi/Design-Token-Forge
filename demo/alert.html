Accessibility

A11y

ARIA roles, keyboard navigation, forced colors, and reduced motion behavior.

ARIA Live Regions

Danger/warning use role="alert" (assertive). Info/success use role="status" (polite). Content is announced automatically by screen readers.

Keyboard

Close button is tabbable and activatable via Enter/Space. Links in body text are focusable. :focus-visible ring on all interactive elements.

Forced Colors

High Contrast mode: border falls back to CanvasText. Accent bar uses Highlight for visibility.

Reduced Motion

When prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active, dismiss transition resolves to near-instant (0.01ms).
