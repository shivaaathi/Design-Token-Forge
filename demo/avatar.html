Shape Gallery

Shape

Circle (default), squircle (25% radius), and rounded-square. Each at the current size.

All 10 density modes. Size, font-size, and icon-size scale proportionally.

Role Colors

Surface

Semantic role colors for the initials/icon fallback background.

Status Badges

Slots

Online, offline, busy, and away indicators. Badge scales with avatar size.

Avatar Group

Layout

Overlapping avatars with ring border. Offset scales per density.

Accessibility

A11y

Focus ring, alt text on images, role="img" with aria-label for initials.

Image Alt

Image avatars require alt attribute with user's name.

Initials ARIA

Use role="img" aria-label="Name" on the avatar container.

Forced Colors

High Contrast shows a visible border on all avatars.
